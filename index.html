Step 1 — Set the reference scale

To convert pixels into real millimeters, the app needs a known distance in your photo.

Place two points on a known feature (a ruler interval, a rivet pitch, a coin diameter) and tell the app how many mm separate them.

Tip: pinch / use mouse wheel to zoom for accurate placement.
